SALES OVERVIEW

what's it for section

Very briefly it is

A/B Risk tool is feature that allows you to reduce risks and move selected postitions from your b-book to liquidity.

Pain Points that're addressed

Successful traders on b-book that make brokerages loose money. Situations of a high volatiltily on a market when it's worth to move currently open positions to a-book from b-book to hedge risks.

Key Business Value

Risks reduction. Increased profitability with b-book offerings.

how it's done part

Implemenation

easy

No implemetation is needed. Works out-of-the box when b-book and liquidities are already set up.

Don't want to re-configure b-book settings yourself?
Apply for TFB Gold Tech Support Package.

Daily Maintenance

average

Manually controlled operation of a feature, client needs to have a deciated specialist who will select accounts/posiitions and move them to preferred liquidities.

Key Technical Limitations

easy

internal

The system doesn't automatically suggests b-book accounts that are worth moving to a-book.

USER GUIDE

Summary

On this page, you may hedge risks by temporarily transferring the trading positions and redirecting all trading acton of selected clients A and B book. For example, you may tranfer B-book clients to A-book for the periods of news publications or high volatility of the market - to minimize your wn risks and probabiility of large losses.

New switch is a section where you can activate a new action of risk management for selected groups and clients.

Active Switches is a section where you can see all currently made transfers that are still active as well as the financial impact of those.

Financial result shows already fixed results of A/B risk tool for transers that have already been returned.

Auto switch allows managing risks immeiately if any events occur. Just set the necessary events as triggers when you create rules.

Active rule is a section where you view all rules of currently activated risk management actions.
